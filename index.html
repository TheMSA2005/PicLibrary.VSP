V Stands for View: Go to Explore ➡️ Select Your Preference/Topic ➡️ See The Pics

S Stands for Save: After Selecting Your Picutre ➡️ Click the Download/Button Below The Picture ➡️ Or If u Hate the Download Button ➡️ Just Right Click the Mouse & Select Save image as...

P Stands for Print: After Viewing The Image of Your Choice ➡️ Press Ctrl+P ➡️ A Pop-Up Will Appear ➡️ Select Your Preference & Also View Additional Settings for more formatting ➡️ Select Print.
